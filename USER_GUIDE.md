# NEXUS Smart Venue System - User Guide

## 🎯 For Fans (Mobile App)

### Getting Started
1. **Open the Fan App** - Your seat info and location are shown in the header
2. **Check Facilities** - Washroom queue times in the "Facilities Status" section
3. **Explore New Features** - Powered by AI for smart recommendations

---

### 🤖 Using the AI Chatbot
**How to Access:**
- Look for the purple "🤖 NEXUS AI" widget in the bottom-right corner of your screen
- Click to open the chat interface

**What You Can Ask:**
- "How long are the wait times?" → Get current queue info
- "What should I order?" → Smart menu recommendations
- "Where's the bathroom?" → Directions and wait times
- "What's recommended?" → AI suggestions based on demand
- "help" → See all available commands

**Features:**
- Real-time responses
- Learns from venue patterns
- Provides personalized suggestions
- Always available during the event

---

### 🧠 AI Smart Queue Advisor
**Located in:** "AI Smart Advisor" section

**What It Does:**
- 📊 Analyzes crowd patterns in real-time
- ⏰ Tells you the BEST time to order food
- 🎯 Shows the shortest queue item
- 📈 Predicts when crowds will decrease

**How to Use It:**
1. Check the "Best Time to Order" card
2. Look at personalized menu suggestions below
3. See which items have the lowest wait times (in green)
4. Click any item to join that queue

**Pro Tip:** Order when the AI says it's green - you'll save 5-10 minutes! ⚡

### 💚 Wellness & Hydration Tracker
**Located in:** "Your Wellness" section

**Three Wellness Categories:**
1. **💧 Stay Hydrated**
   - Click to log when you drink water
   - AI reminds you every 15 minutes
   - Track your hydration cups logged

2. **🪑 Break Time**
   - Suggests regular breaks during the event
   - Helps maintain energy levels
   - Click to log break time

3. **⚡ Energy Level**
   - Real-time energy percentage (0-100%)
   - Updates based on your activities
   - Goal: Keep above 75% for best experience

**Why It Matters:**
- Prevents dehydration at the venue
- Helps you make it through the whole event without fatigue
- Improves your overall experience

### 🔮 Predictive Crowd Alerts
**What It Shows:**
- Crowd predictions for the NEXT 15 minutes
- Specific locations likely to get crowded
- Severity levels (🔴 HIGH, 🟡 MEDIUM)
- Recommended actions

**Example:**
```
In 5 min → Sector K (Restrooms) → HIGH 🔴 → Deploy staff
In 10 min → Gate 3 (Main Exit) → MEDIUM 🟡 → Monitor
In 15 min → Concourse B (Food) → HIGH 🔴 → Increase cashiers
```

**How to Use:**
1. Glance at predictions every 5-10 minutes
2. Pro-actively deploy staff before crowds form
3. Prevent bottlenecks before they happen
4. Improve overall guest experience

### 📱 Broadcasting to Fans

1. Select alert type:
